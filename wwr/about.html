Rafting Travel

We exist to bring adventure to life. Our mission is to create unforgettable rafting experiences filled with excitement, teamwork, and breathtaking natural beauty.As you glide across calm waters, each paddle stroke feels like a rhythmic meditation, allowing you to connect deeply with nature. You can explore hidden coves, watch birds up close, and feel the soothing embrace of the water — all while strengthening your body and refreshing your mind. It’s both an adventure and a peaceful escape in one.

History

In the rugged mountains of queens town New Zealand, Rafting Travel was founded by a band of misfit friends after they accidentally launched a raft off a cliff into the shotover river, with a barbacue still sizzling on board. Surviving the wild ride, they declared ,'This is a business idea!' Rafting Travel was born in a crazy, edge-of-reason trips on the world's most scenic rivers. From Leaping waterfalls to dodging boulders, their motto at a time was "Thrill guaranteed, Safety For sure" Now adventurers worldwide chase sunsets and rapids with rafting travel.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted company delivering thrilling rafting experiences.
